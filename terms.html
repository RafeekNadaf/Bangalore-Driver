Our Policy

  • The Customer has to give the one day prior notice for the drivers service booking.
  • All bookings have to be made at least 4hrs in case of emergency for proper facilitation of the service.
  • When the customer completes the 4hrs time, we provide another 15mins which is not chargeable, but after 15mins the customer has to pay an hour charges to the company, either they may use it or not.
  • We are not responsible for any short of direct booking made to driver when the customer contacts the driver directly, in such cases the company will not provide you any service next time.
  • Each booking will be followed up with a confirmation call or SMS to your registered mobile number depending on availability of drivers and proximity of reporting location.
  • The company is not liable for any damages caused due to accidents that might have occurred during the duty time of our drivers. The company follows a well laid down due diligence / vetting process for the recruitment of its drivers.
  • Any cancellation of a booking by the customer has to be made 2 hours prior to the start of the booking. Otherwise a cancellation charge of Rs.200/- will be applicable.
  • If the driver demand excess amount on our charges just call us immediately.
  • Rs.250/- should be paid extra per day as food allowances for Out Station Trips.
  • Drivers will not wash the car.
  • Please do not insist or encourage the driver for over speed.
  • Do not leave cash / valuables in possession of the drivers on vehicles.
  • Payment should be made by the customers directly to the drivers.
