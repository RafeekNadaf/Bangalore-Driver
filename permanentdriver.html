    Permanent Driver Service

    A 'permanent driver' is a personal driver who will report to you on daily basis at your set reporting time for which he will draw a monthly salary. We offer three services under permanent services

    Classic Recruitment

    A- 3 month membership @ Rs. 3500 plus service tax.

    What you will get,

  • 1. The driver will be recruited based on your requirement.
  • 2. The driver will be verified from our side ( reference check both previous employer and social circle &document check)
  • 3. The driver will be trained in soft skills from our in house training program; you can always send your driver to attend the refresher training if you wish (no extra charge).
  • 4. You can replace the driver in case you are not satisfied with his service, we will replace another driver in his place at no extra charge.
  • 5. In case your driver is on leave you can avail a temporary driver at discounted rates.

    • Note :- You may always increase your membership by paying a nominal fee so that you are always with a driver to serve you. Screening and recruiting driver for you is taken care off by us in case the driver leaves the job.
