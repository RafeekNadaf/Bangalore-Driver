About our Company

‘Bangalore Drivers 24/7’ provides residents of Bangalore with a dependable, efficient and safe ‘driver-on-call’ service. Enjoy a hassle-free commute and make your travel time more productive – and leave the stress of driving to us.

Bangalore Drivers 24/7 is a premiere company in Bangalore which exclusively deals in car driver placement & training, we provide drivers to individual car owners and corporate companies having fleet of drivers.

We provide customized driver services for various requirement like,Driver on agreement (for individual & corporate). Drivers on Contract (only for corporate). Temporary drivers( for Individual & Corporate). Valet parking, outstation driving ,events, night outs.

'Bangalore Drivers 24/7' was started with a team of 4 people with a vision to make it real BIG in Driver recruitment space. Mr. Sonu (MD) & founder has worked as a driver for 9 years in Bangalore he understands the problems faced by drivers and car owners. There are many placement agencies but they are not specialized in car driver staffing, they always have a limited database with them.

We work differently by having a different approach:

  • Step 1: Before the driver gets placement through us he has to come for an one to one interaction in our company with his ful documents.
  • Step 2: If the driver seems to be fit in initial screening and have valid documents then he need to fill a form mentioning all his details and references.
  • Step 3: After the driver fills the form a reference call is made to cross verify and after this a driver is called in for 4 hours training session which covers various aspect of what a good driver should do.
  • Step 4:Once the driver has successfully completed the training program his document are forwarded to the recruitment team for considering him for Employement through us.
  • Step 5: After we get a requirement to hire a driver the detailed are taken down by our executive and after that the driver is shortlisted as per the requirement and send for a trail and interview with the client.
  • Step 6: Once the driver is placed we take feedback from client on driver’s performance and incase the client is not satisfied with the driver we replace the driver in consultation with the client.
